LIVE STREAMING

MEET MEVO

To your attendees, the Mevo will look like a studio microphone in front of the stage, but its small size belies its capabilities. Seated nearby will be a Video Producer with an iPad controlling your live broadcast. From the iPad, the Producer will be able to create multiple camera angles and cut between them while adding slides and lower-third titles as if it were TV.

STREAM WHERE YOU WANT

We can stream directly to all of the most popular social media platforms. In fact, we can stream to multiple social media channels at the same time, so your audience can find you wherever they happen to be.

GRAPHICS

Lower-Third Titles

Lower-Third Titles are perfect for broadcasting the names and titles of your on-screen talent. Font and background colors can be customized for your brand.

Logo Corner Bug

Brand your live stream so viewers know who is broadcasting. The corner bug can be turned on and off throughout the duration of your live stream.

Presentation Slides

No more trying to make a video of your projected slides look good; we can cue a deck of your slides before the broadcast so they can be displayed throughout the live stream.
